X-RAG — Datasets
================

The raw datasets are NOT stored in this repository. They total ~320 MB, are
third-party data under their own licenses, and are meant to be downloaded rather
than re-hosted. The pipeline fetches them from the Hugging Face Hub on demand:

    python -m training.data.download_datasets     # download all of the below
    python -m training.data.normalize             # -> data/train, data/test JSONL

(Or just run section 2 of run_full_pipeline.ipynb.)

Sources
-------

Generator fine-tuning (SFT)
  HAGRID        miracl/hagrid       https://huggingface.co/datasets/miracl/hagrid

NLI attribution head (entailment calibration)
  ANLI          facebook/anli       https://huggingface.co/datasets/facebook/anli
  FEVER v1.0    fever/fever         https://huggingface.co/datasets/fever/fever
  WICE          jon-tow/wice        https://huggingface.co/datasets/jon-tow/wice
  VitaminC      tals/vitaminc       https://huggingface.co/datasets/tals/vitaminc

(The NLI head loads FEVER via its NLI-reformatted mirror, pietrolesci/nli_fever.)
